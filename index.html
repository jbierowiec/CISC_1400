About Me

I am a Junior at Fordham University Studying Engineering Physics and pursuing a minor in Computer Sciecne. On most days I like to learn new things, program, and develop new things. During my time of leisure I like to play chess, cycle, and hike (Hence the mountain above).

Aside from being STEM oriented I am a big history buff, specifically for World War II history. For a long time now, I have been thinking about how to combine computer science, animation, and history all in one. History, like most humanities, is a subject based on reading and writing. Learning or teaching it in a fun way however, might make more students interested in the subject.

My favorite video that protrays this is by IPNtvPL. This video provides a brief summary of all the events that happened in Polish history at the start of the Second Wolrd War to the fall of Communism. This video perfectly wraps computer science, animation, and history because in its core the video was made using C or C#, then using either Blender or Unity, the graphics of the video were made, and lastly a historic storyline was created.



The goal for the future is the learn C#, Unity, and read up on some history to make similar videos in more diverse time periods.
